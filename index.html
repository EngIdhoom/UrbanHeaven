Who are we?

Your Modern Living Experience in Nairobi, The Green City in the Sun!

Welcome to Urban Heaven Apartments, where luxury, comfort, and convenience come together in the heart of Nairobi, The Green City in the Sun. Whether you're looking for a short-term retreat or a long-term residence, our fully furnished, high-end apartments offer the perfect blend of modern elegance and urban sophistication.


Choose from a range of stylish living spaces:


  • 4BR & 3BR - Spacious, luxurious, and perfect for families or executives
  • 2BR & 1BR - Ideal for professionals and couples seeking comfort and style
  • Studio apartments - Sleek, smart, and designed for urban efficiency

At Urban Heaven Apartments, we redefine modern city living with breathtaking views, top-tier amenities, and a seamless rental experience. Whether you're in Nairobi for work, leisure, or a fresh start, your perfect home awaits. Book your stay today!
